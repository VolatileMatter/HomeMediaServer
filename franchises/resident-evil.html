📖 History of Resident Evil
1996–1999
Birth of Survival Horror
Shinji Mikami directed RE1 on PS1, inventing the genre. RE2 became a cultural phenomenon with 5M+ sales. Fixed cameras, limited saves, puzzle-box mansion.
2005–2012
Action Era & RE4 Revolution
RE4 redefined third-person shooting. RE5 became Capcom's best-selling game (14M+). RE6 oversaturated with action.
2017–Present
Horror Renaissance
RE7 brought back horror in first-person. RE2 Remake and RE4 Remake cemented the series as Capcom's flagship. Cumulative sales >150 million.
