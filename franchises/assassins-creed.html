🏛️ History of the Assassin's Creed Franchise
2004–2007 — Conception

Originally intended as a Prince of Persia spin-off, Patrice Désilets turned it into a new IP. AC1 launched Nov 2007 with critical acclaim but mixed mission structure.

2009–2011 — Ezio Renaissance

ACII turned the series into a pop-culture titan. Brotherhood introduced multiplayer; Revelations closed Ezio's story. Ubisoft annualized the franchise.

2012–2015 — Expansion & Controversy

ACIII, Black Flag, Unity, Rogue. Unity’s buggy launch (2014) damaged reputation; Syndicate (2015) underperformed, leading to a hiatus.

2017–2020 — RPG Revolution

Origins introduced RPG mechanics, Odyssey (2018) expanded, Valhalla (2020) became the highest-grossing AC game. The Discovery Tour mode won praise from educators.

2023–present — Return to Roots & Future

Mirage (2023) brought back stealth. Upcoming titles: AC Codename Red (Feudal Japan, 2025), Codename Hexe, and Netflix animated series in development.
