About the Franchise

Final Fantasy is a science fiction and fantasy anthology media franchise created by Hironobu Sakaguchi and developed by Square (now Square Enix). The franchise centers on a series of role-playing video games, but has expanded into films, anime, manga, novels, audio dramas, and other media. Each main installment is typically set in a different universe with distinct characters and stories, though recurring elements include chocobos, moogles, magic, crystals, and characters named Cid.
