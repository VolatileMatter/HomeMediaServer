Welcome to the Home Server 🐉

You've been invited to access a private, self-hosted media and gaming server. Everything here is owned and operated by us — no ads, no tracking by third parties, no subscriptions for you. We've paid for and legally own all the media and games on this server. This is our answer to streaming services removing content, region-locking, or shutting down.

All you need is a Google account (Gmail). Your server host will add your email to the access list — after that, everything works with a single "Sign in with Google" click.

The server tracks some activity (what you've watched, read, or played) to power the achievement system and give you personalized recommendations. This is all stored privately on the server — it never leaves the building. You can opt out at any time in User Settings → Privacy.

🚀 Getting Started — Step by Step
1
Tell your host your Gmail address

Send your full Gmail address (e.g. yourname@gmail.com) to the server owner. They'll add you to the access list. This usually takes a few minutes.

2
Visit the server homepage

Open home.yourdomain.com in any browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge — all work). You'll see a page with a "Sign in with Google" button.

3
Sign in with your Google account

Click the button, choose your Google account, and you're in. You don't create a separate password — your Google account IS your login. If you're already signed into Google in your browser, it's one click.

4
Your homepage — Jellyfin is already there

Your homepage shows a weather widget, the time, any news from the server owner, and a "Continue Watching" section from Jellyfin. Start browsing movies and TV shows right away.

🎮 Set up game streaming (one-time, per device)

To play games streamed from the server, install Moonlight on your device — free, takes about 2 minutes. The server owner will pair your device the first time.

Your deviceWhere to get Moonlight
Windows / Mac / Linuxmoonlight-stream.org
AndroidGoogle Play — "Moonlight Game Streaming"
iPhone / iPadApp Store — "Moonlight Game Streaming"

Once paired: open Moonlight → tap the server → tap Game Library. You'll see the full game catalogue in a fullscreen launcher. Pick a game and play.

💡 How it works: The game runs on the server — your device just receives the video and sends your controller input back. You need a gamepad (USB or Bluetooth) on your end.
Explore the App Library to add more services

Click the "⊞ App Library" button on your homepage. You'll see every available service — audiobooks, music, comics, games, AI chat, and more. Toggle on the ones you want. They'll appear in your feed and quick-access links.

7
Customize in User Settings

Visit settings.yourdomain.com to set your ZIP code for weather, choose temperature units, control what sections appear on your homepage, and manage your privacy preferences — including whether your activity is tracked for achievements and recommendations.

🏆 Achievements

The server has an achievement system! As you read books, watch movies, play games, and listen to audiobooks, you earn XP and unlock achievements. You can see the community feed on your homepage — who earned what recently.

For retro games, you can connect your RetroAchievements.org account in User Settings → RetroAchievements. Your in-game achievements will sync to your RA profile and appear in the server feed.

Control your privacy in User Settings → Privacy. You can make specific achievements private, hide your activity from the community feed, or opt out of activity tracking entirely.

🛡️ Opting Out

You can opt out of activity tracking (used for achievements and recommendations) at any time. Go to settings.yourdomain.com → Privacy → Activity Tracking and toggle it off. When opted out, no new activity data is collected and the recommendation engine will not update your profile. Previously collected data can be deleted on request.

❓ FAQ
Do I need to install anything?
No. Everything works in a web browser. For the best TV experience with Jellyfin, the free Jellyfin app is recommended but optional. For photo backup (Immich) and audiobooks on mobile, install the respective apps.
Is my data private?
Yes. Everything is self-hosted on a private server in someone's home. Your viewing history, reading progress, and save files are stored locally and not shared with any company. The server owner can see aggregate usage but does not monitor individual activity.
What's my storage limit?
Your Files (Nextcloud) quota is set by the server owner. It covers both your regular file uploads AND your app data (game saves, progress exports, etc.). Check your current usage in Nextcloud → Files → Storage indicator.
Can I access it from anywhere?
Yes — but you need Tailscale installed on your device first. Once Tailscale is running and you've been approved by the server owner, all services are accessible from anywhere via *.ixo.lol addresses. Smart TVs (LG, Roku, Fire TV, Tizen) use a different Jellyfin address — ask the server owner for that URL.
Can I share my account with family?
Accounts are per-person — your Google account is your login. If a family member wants access, the server owner can add their Gmail address. This keeps saves, progress, and achievements separate per person.
Something isn't working — what do I do?
Use the Feedback button on the homepage (or message the server owner directly). Include what service, what you were doing, and what happened. Screenshots help.
Why doesn't [service] show on my homepage?
New services don't appear automatically — they're opt-in to keep your homepage clean. Go to the App Library (⊞ button on homepage) and enable the services you want.
