Welcome to the Home Server 🐉

You've been invited to access a private, self-hosted media and gaming server. Everything here is owned and operated by us — no ads, no tracking, no subscriptions for you. We've paid for and legally own all the media and games on this server. This is our answer to streaming services removing content, region locking, or shutting down.

All you need is a Google account (Gmail). Your server host will add your email to the access list — after that, everything works with a single "Sign in with Google" click.

🚀 Getting Started — Step by Step
1
Tell your host your Gmail address

Send your full Gmail address (e.g. [email protected]) to the server owner. They'll add you to the access list. This usually takes a few minutes.

2
Visit the server homepage

Open home.yourdomain.com in any browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge — all work). You'll see a blank screen with a "Sign in with Google" button.

3
Sign in with your Google account

Click the button, choose your Google account, and you're in. You don't create a separate password. Your Google account IS your login. If you're already signed into Google in your browser, it's one click.

4
You'll see your homepage — Jellyfin is already there

Your homepage shows a weather widget, the time, news from the server owner, and a "Continue Watching" section from Jellyfin (the movie and TV server). Start browsing movies and TV shows right away.

5
Explore the App Library to add more services

Click the "⊞ App Library" button near the top of your homepage. You'll see every available service — audiobooks, music, comics, games, AI chat, and more. Toggle on the ones you want. They'll appear in your feed and quick-access links.

6
Customize your homepage in User Settings

Visit settings.yourdomain.com to set your ZIP code for weather, choose temperature units, control what sections appear on your homepage, and configure your privacy preferences for achievements.

🏆 Achievements

The server has an achievement system! As you read books, watch movies, play games, and listen to audiobooks, you earn achievements. You can see the community feed on your homepage — who earned what recently.

For retro games, you can connect your RetroAchievements.org account in User Settings → RetroAchievements. Your in-game achievements will sync to your RA profile and appear in the server feed.

Control your privacy in User Settings → Privacy — you can make specific achievements private if you don't want them in the community feed.

❓ FAQ
Do I need to install anything?
No. Everything works in a web browser. For the best TV experience with Jellyfin, the free Jellyfin app is recommended but optional. For photo backup (Immich), install the Immich mobile app.
Is my data private?
Yes. Everything is self-hosted on a private server. Your viewing history, reading progress, and save files are stored locally and not shared with any company. Only you and the server owner can see your data.
What's my storage limit?
Your Files (Nextcloud) quota is set by the server owner — it covers both your regular file uploads AND your app data (game saves, progress exports, etc.). Check your storage in Nextcloud → Files → Storage indicator.
Can I access it away from home?
Yes! All services are accessible from anywhere via the yourdomain.com addresses. You need an internet connection but it works on mobile data.
Something isn't working — who do I contact?
Check the Owner News section on your homepage for any known issues. Otherwise, contact the server owner directly. The server has uptime monitoring so the owner may already know about issues.
Can I request that specific media be added?
Ask the server owner! The seedbox can automatically download TV shows and movies, and Readarr can auto-grab books by specific authors. New releases appear within hours of becoming available.
How do I log out?
Visit auth.yourdomain.com and click logout. Or simply close the browser — sessions are persistent until you explicitly log out or the server owner revokes access.
