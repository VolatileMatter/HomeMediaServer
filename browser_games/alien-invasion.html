🎮 What Is This?

Alien Invasion is a Space Invaders-style arcade shooter built on HTML5 Canvas. Rows of aliens move back and forth in formation, descending one row each time they hit the screen edge. You control a ship at the bottom, shooting upward while dodging the aliens' return fire from behind shields that erode with each hit.

The canonical tension of Space Invaders is intact: as you reduce the formation, the survivors speed up — and at some point the remaining few are moving so fast the game tips from controlled into frantic. That speed escalation is the heart of the genre and Alien Invasion delivers it.

It's a natural high-score competition game — runs are short (5–15 minutes), the difficulty curve is consistent enough that scores are comparable between players, and the single-variable metric (score) makes leaderboard talk simple.

📖 How to Play
  • Move the ship left and right to avoid alien fire
  • Shoot upward to destroy the alien formation
  • Shields protect you initially but erode with hits — don't hide behind them for too long
  • Aliens descend faster as their numbers reduce — the last few are the most dangerous
  • Clear all aliens to advance to the next wave
  • Lose all lives and the run ends
