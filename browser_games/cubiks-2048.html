🎮 What Is This?

Cubiks-2048 takes the 2048 sliding tile mechanic and maps it onto the six faces of a 3D cube rendered in Three.js. The cube can be rotated to view and interact with all faces. Swiping in a direction slides all tiles on the visible face in that direction — tiles of equal value merge as in the original 2048 — but the 3D geometry means the consequences of a move affect multiple faces in a way that's harder to predict than on a flat grid.

The added spatial dimension substantially increases the difficulty. Players familiar with 2048 strategy will find their flat-grid intuitions partially transfer but frequently mislead in three dimensions. Corner strategies become far more complex when the "corners" wrap around a cube. It's a clever spatial puzzle that rewards the same type of careful planning as 2048 but demands significantly more geometric reasoning.

📖 How to Play
  • Drag to rotate the cube and view different faces
  • Swipe (or use arrow keys) on a face to slide all tiles in that direction
  • Matching tiles merge into their sum when they collide
  • A new tile appears after each move
  • Goal: reach 2048 (or higher) on any tile
  • Game ends when no moves remain on any face
Controls
Drag Rotate the cube to view different faces Swipe Slide tiles on the current face Arrow keys Slide tiles (keyboard alternative)
