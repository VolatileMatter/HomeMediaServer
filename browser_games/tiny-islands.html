🎮 What Is This?

Tiny Islands is a procedural terrain generator focused specifically on island maps. Each generation produces a self-contained island with varying topography: mountains, forests, beaches, shallow water, deep ocean, and small settlements or boats placed naturally within the landscape.

Created by Marian42 (a prolific generative art developer), it uses layered noise functions and rule-based placement to ensure islands always feel geographically plausible — mountains appear inland, forests grow on lower slopes, villages sit near the coast. The style is minimal and charming, rendered in flat colours with a top-down map aesthetic.

It is purely a generator — there is no interaction beyond clicking to regenerate. The value is in the endless variety and the occasional island that feels genuinely special. Some players keep the tab open and hit regenerate whenever they want a moment of calm.

📖 How to Use
  • Open the page — an island generates immediately
  • Click anywhere (or the Regenerate button) to produce a new island
  • There are no settings or controls beyond generation
  • To save a favourite, take a screenshot — there is no built-in export
