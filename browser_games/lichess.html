♟️ What Is This?

Lichess is the world's most-used free chess server, handling millions of games per day. It is entirely ad-free and funded by voluntary donations. Every feature — from Stockfish engine analysis to game broadcasts of titled tournaments — is available for free to every user.

The software behind it is Lila, a Scala application developed in the open on GitHub. The source code is released under AGPL-3.0. Lichess has become a symbol of what open-source, non-commercial software can achieve.

Key features include: rated online play across all time controls (Bullet, Blitz, Rapid, Classical, Correspondence), daily puzzles, a full Stockfish analysis board, study tools for annotation and lesson-building, Puzzle Storm/Racer mini-games, simultaneous exhibitions, and live broadcasts of professional events.

The official iOS and Android apps are also open-source and connect to the same server. No special account is needed to use them beyond a lichess.org login.

⌨️ Controls

Drag-and-drop or click-to-move pieces in the browser. Keyboard shortcuts are available for analysis: ←/→ to step through moves, f to flip the board, space to play the best engine move. Full controls documented at lichess.org/faq.
