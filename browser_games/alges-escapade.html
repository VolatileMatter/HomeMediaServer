🎮 What Is This?

Alge's Escapade was created by Dave-and-Mike for GitHub's Game Off 2012 game jam — a month-long competition with the theme "fork." The entry centres on Alge, a small sea creature navigating ocean-themed levels rendered in charming pixel-art style on HTML5 Canvas.

It's a platformer in the classic sense: run, jump, collect, avoid. What sets it apart from many jam games is the quality of execution — polished artwork, responsive controls, and a complete level set that tells a coherent little story rather than stopping abruptly after one or two rooms. The ocean aesthetic is consistently applied throughout: coral platforms, bubbles, fish enemies, and a colour palette that feels genuinely aquatic.

The GitHub Game Off archive is full of forgotten gems like this — games that were ambitious for a jam entry, got attention briefly at submission, then drifted into obscurity. Surfacing them in the hub gives them the audience they deserved.
