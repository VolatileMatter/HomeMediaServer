🎮 What Is This?

C4 is a clean, no-frills browser implementation of Connect Four — the classic two-player strategy game where you drop coloured discs into a 7×6 vertical grid and try to line up four in a row before your opponent does. The standout feature over other browser Connect Four implementations is the built-in AI: it uses the minimax algorithm with alpha-beta pruning, making higher difficulty settings genuinely difficult to beat.

The game supports both human vs AI and local two-player (pass-and-play on the same device) modes. AI difficulty is configurable — lower settings play randomly or near-randomly, higher settings search deeper in the game tree and will punish most mistakes. Maximum difficulty is essentially unbeatable for casual players.

Games last 2–5 minutes, making it a good quick-session game. The compact, readable codebase also makes it a nice reference for anyone curious about implementing minimax in JavaScript.

📖 How to Play
  • Select a game mode (vs AI or vs Human) and AI difficulty level
  • Click a column to drop your disc into the lowest available row
  • Connect four discs in a row — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal — to win
  • The AI responds immediately after your move
  • The board fills up — if nobody connects four before the grid is full, it's a draw

🏆 Leaderboard

There is no scoring system beyond win/loss per game. A win streak counter could be added by patching the game-over callback to POST to the hub's score API, but this is low priority given the pass-and-play nature of the two-player mode.
