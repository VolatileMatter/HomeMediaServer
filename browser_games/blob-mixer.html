🎮 What Is This?

Blob Mixer is an ambient canvas animation where soft, rounded shapes drift around the canvas, their edges continuously morphing. When blobs overlap, their colours mix. When they drift apart, they separate back into individual forms. The movement is governed by gentle physics — no sharp edges, no sudden changes, just slow organic drift.

This toy is from the 100 Days of Code visual toys series — a project where developers commit to creating one small, focused browser art experiment each day for 100 days. Blob Mixer is one of the series' more serene entries, designed as something you'd leave running in the background.

It is purely passive — there are no interactions beyond possibly clicking to add more blobs or change colour settings (check the specific build for controls). The aesthetic is soft and meditative.
